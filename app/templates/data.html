🧠 Context Sentence Backfill

Words without a context sentence fall back to regular recall mode instead of cloze. Click once — runs automatically until all words are filled. You can stop and resume anytime; progress is saved per word.

Requires AI (OpenAI or Ollama) to be running. Each batch of 50 takes ~10–60 seconds. Already-filled words are never touched.

💾 About Backups

This app follows a local-first philosophy. Your data stays on your device.

  • Use Create backup now above to save a full snapshot (data + scores) anytime
  • Automatic backups are created at app startup and before each import
  • Backups are stored in the data/backups/ folder
  • We recommend exporting or creating a backup regularly for additional safety
  • Export files are human-readable JSON for transparency
